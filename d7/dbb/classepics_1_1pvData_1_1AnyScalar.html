Detailed Description

A type-safe variant union capable of holding any of the PVD scalar types (POD or string)

AnyScalar A(5);
assert(A.type()==pvInt);
assert(A.ref<int32>()==5);
assert(A.as<int32>()==5);
assert(A.as<double>()==5.0);
assert(A.ref<double>()==5.0); // throws AnyScalar::bad_cast
